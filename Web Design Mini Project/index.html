OUR STORY

Pierre Draps

Godiva’s founder Pierre Draps created delicious chocolates until his very last days in 2012.
Our chef chocolatiers are proud to follow in his footsteps and keep the legacy of fine Belgian chocolates alive, and so the Godiva story continues…

ABOUT US

At Godiva we support sustainable practices and programs on a worldwide basis.
We strive to invest in programs and practices that lead to lasting positive outcomes for our consumers, partners and communities.
We are proud of the work that we do to support farmers and our partners, safeguard our environment, and empower the communities in which we work and live.
